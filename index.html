Career tracks and Support

UI/UX Design

They are generally responsible for collecting, researching, investigating and evaluating user requirements. They are to deliver an outstanding user experience providing an exceptional and intuitive application design

Front End development

They determine the structure and design of web pages, striking a balance between functional and aesthetic design and ensuring web design is optimized for smartphones.

Mobile development

They specialise in mobile technology such as building apps for Google's Android, Apple's iOS and Microsoft's Windows Phone platforms. Their tracks include Android developer and iOS developer.

Back End development

They are responsible for server-side web application logic and integration of the work front-end web developers do.

Desktop Application development

They oversee the entire life cycle of a software application. They design and create the application, code it, and then test the product for functionality and errors
